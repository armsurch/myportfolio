About Me

ICT Professional Supporting Nigeria's Digital Infrastructure and Government Systems

I'm Armstrong Nzotta, an ICT professional passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world challenges. My journey began at Abia State University (ABSU) where I earned my degree in Information and Communication Technology, and has led me to supporting critical infrastructure for Nigeria's judiciary system.

Current Role

Currently serving as IT Support specialist at Lextech Eco System Limited, I ensure seamless operation of IT systems that support legal processes and administrative functions within the Nigerian Judiciary. My role involves troubleshooting hardware, software, network, and connectivity issues while maintaining the security and reliability that judicial operations demand.

Government Experience

My career has been defined by hands-on experience in government digitalization initiatives. During my tenure at Lloydant Business Services, I played a pivotal role in deploying e-Government and digital identity programs across Enugu State, directly impacting thousands of citizens through secure biometric enrollment systems and robust network infrastructure.

I believe technology should simplify complex processes and bridge gaps in service delivery. Whether I'm configuring MikroTik routers for secure government networks, training staff on biometric systems, or troubleshooting critical judiciary IT infrastructure, my focus remains on creating reliable, secure solutions that serve real people with real needs.
